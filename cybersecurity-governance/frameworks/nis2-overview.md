# NIS2 Directive Overview

## Purpose

The NIS2 Directive strengthens cybersecurity requirements for essential and important entities across the European Union.

It aims to improve:

- cybersecurity risk management
- incident reporting
- resilience of critical infrastructure
- supply chain security

---

## Key Requirements

Organizations must implement:

- risk management measures
- incident response capabilities
- supply chain security controls
- vulnerability handling
- security monitoring

---

## Governance Responsibilities

Management bodies must:

- approve cybersecurity risk management measures
- oversee implementation
- ensure compliance with regulatory requirements

---

## Security Measures

Typical measures include:

- access control policies
- encryption
- incident detection
- business continuity planning
- vulnerability management

---

## Key Takeaway

NIS2 moves cybersecurity from purely technical responsibility to **organizational governance responsibility**.
