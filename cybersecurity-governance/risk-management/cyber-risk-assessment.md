# Cyber Risk Assessment

## Objective

A cyber risk assessment identifies and evaluates risks related to information systems, data, and digital infrastructure.

---

## Basic Risk Model

Risk can be expressed as:

Risk = Likelihood × Impact

---

## Risk Assessment Steps

1. Identify assets
2. Identify threats
3. Identify vulnerabilities
4. Evaluate likelihood
5. Evaluate potential impact
6. Determine risk level
7. Define mitigation measures

---

## Example Risk Categories

- unauthorized access
- data breaches
- service disruption
- supply chain compromise
- insider threats

---

## Mitigation Strategies

Typical mitigation measures include:

- access control
- network segmentation
- monitoring and detection
- backup strategies
- employee awareness training
