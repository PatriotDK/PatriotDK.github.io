# NIS2 Compliance Checklist

## Governance

- [ ] Cybersecurity risk management policy established
- [ ] Management oversight of cybersecurity measures

---

## Risk Management

- [ ] Risk assessment process implemented
- [ ] Vulnerability management program established

---

## Incident Handling

- [ ] Incident response plan documented
- [ ] Incident reporting procedures defined

---

## Supply Chain Security

- [ ] Vendor risk assessment process
- [ ] Security requirements for suppliers

---

## Security Controls

- [ ] Access control mechanisms
- [ ] Encryption where appropriate
- [ ] Logging and monitoring
