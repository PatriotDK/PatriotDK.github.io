# Vendor Security Questionnaire

## Purpose

This questionnaire helps assess the security posture of third-party vendors.

---

## Security Governance

- Do you have a formal information security policy?
- Who is responsible for cybersecurity in your organization?

---

## Access Control

- Do you use multi-factor authentication?
- How are user privileges managed?

---

## Data Protection

- How is sensitive data protected?
- Do you encrypt data in transit and at rest?

---

## Incident Response

- Do you have an incident response plan?
- How quickly are customers notified in case of a breach?

---

## Compliance

- Are you compliant with any standards? (ISO 27001, SOC 2, etc.)
