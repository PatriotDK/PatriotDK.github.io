# Cyber Risk Register Template

## Overview

A cyber risk register helps organizations track and manage cybersecurity risks in a structured way.

---

## Example Structure

| Risk ID | Risk Description | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Level | Mitigation | Owner | Status |
|--------|-----------------|-----------|--------|-----------|-----------|------|------|
| R001 | Unauthorized system access | Medium | High | High | MFA implementation | IT Security | Open |
| R002 | Data breach from phishing | High | High | Critical | Security awareness training | Compliance | Open |
| R003 | Third-party vendor compromise | Medium | High | High | Vendor security assessment | Procurement | Monitoring |

---

## Key Elements

A good risk register includes:

- clear risk descriptions
- likelihood and impact assessment
- mitigation strategies
- responsible owners
- review status
