# AI Regulation and Governance

## Overview

This section documents notes, frameworks, and structured reflections related to **AI regulation, governance, compliance, and operational risk management**.

The purpose of this repository is to explore how organizations can use AI responsibly while managing legal, ethical, and operational risks.

My interest in this area sits at the intersection of:

- law
- technology
- cybersecurity
- compliance
- governance
- digital transformation

---

## Focus Areas

This repository includes notes and examples related to:

- AI regulation
- governance frameworks
- risk assessment
- accountability and oversight
- transparency and documentation
- operational implementation of AI controls

---

## Topics Covered

Key themes include:

- EU AI Act
- GDPR and AI
- human oversight
- high-risk AI systems
- AI governance models
- documentation and traceability
- vendor and third-party AI risk
- responsible use of AI in organizations

---

## Objectives

The purpose of this repository is to:

- structure knowledge about AI regulation and governance
- connect legal requirements with practical implementation
- document governance approaches for responsible AI use
- explore how AI risk can be assessed and managed
- build a practical reference for AI compliance and oversight
