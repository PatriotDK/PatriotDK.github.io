# AI Governance Checklist

## Before Using an AI System

- [ ] Define the purpose of the system
- [ ] Identify whether personal or sensitive data is involved
- [ ] Assess legal and regulatory implications
- [ ] Review security considerations
- [ ] Clarify accountability and ownership
- [ ] Determine whether human oversight is required
- [ ] Document the use case
- [ ] Assess third-party vendor risk
- [ ] Define approval process
- [ ] Plan review and monitoring

---

## After Deployment

- [ ] Monitor outputs and performance
- [ ] Review incidents or errors
- [ ] Reassess risk periodically
- [ ] Update documentation
- [ ] Confirm continued suitability of the system
